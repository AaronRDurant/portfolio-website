About

I'm a full-stack web developer in Michigan.

After a few years traversing across North America writing about motor sports as an accredited journalist, I happened upon web development late in the summer of 2020.

My experience writing for and maintaining online publications gave me an abundance of soft skills and even a few related hard skills that helped as I began coding, but I had a lot to learn.

As a project-based online bootcamp, CareerFoundry's Full-Stack Web Development Program got me up to speed on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript before introducing me to Node.js, React, Angular, and so much more in between. Alongside the valuable structure of the program, I also took my learning into my own hands, diving deeper into certain concepts and beginning to tinker with personal projects to further prepare for my first role in the industry.

Beyond coding, I enjoy snowboarding, mountain biking, running, playing the drums, reading books, and watching motocross vlogs.
